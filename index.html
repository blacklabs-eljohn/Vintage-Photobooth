RetroLens — The Vintage Photobooth

Step inside our digital time machine to create nostalgic vintage photostrips, polaroids, and postcard captures instantly.

Features of RetroLens Digital Photobooth

  • Classic Formats: Create nostalgic 4-panel Photo Strips, Retro Polaroids, Cinematic Frame Cards, and Custom Postcards.
  • Authentic Retro Styling: Customize your photos with realistic film grain, analog light leaks, dust & scratches overlay effects, and custom classic borders.
  • Live Capture & Digital Negatives: Use your device camera to take live shots with a visual shutter count-down and flash effect, or upload digital photo negatives.
  • Co-Op Duet Capture: Connect with a friend in real-time to take matching photostrip photos side-by-side.
  • Download and Share: Generate high-resolution digital prints to save and share your vintage moments instantly.

How it works:

  1. Choose your preferred layout format on the console keypad (Strip, Polaroid, Cine, or Postcard).
  2. Credit the machine by dropping a vintage 1-peso coin into the coin entry slot.
  3. Select Solo mode for a personal photoshoot or Duet mode to host/join a remote session.
  4. Step inside! Allow camera access or upload your photo files.
  5. Choose filters (B&W, Sepia, Retro Warm, Cold Slate, etc.), adjust settings, write a caption, and sign your print.
  6. Dispense your physical vintage photostrip print!
